Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/1/2025

ClosetMax respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos you upload (for wardrobe analysis)
  • Location data (to show nearby donation centers and weather updates)
  • User-provided data like style preferences and clothing descriptions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Analyze clothing items and generate outfit recommendations
  • Provide local weather forecasts
  • Suggest nearby donation centers
  • Improve your experience within the app

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • To use third-party services like Google Cloud Vision API and Apple WeatherKit for analysis and features
  • When required by law

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Delete uploaded photos at any time
  • Disable location access in your device settings

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any significant changes.
